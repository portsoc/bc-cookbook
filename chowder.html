Frankfurter and sweetcorn chowder

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 4 Herta pork franfurters (or similar)
  • 200g tinned sweetcorn
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • Milk
  • 1 pork stock cube
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Cooking oil

Method

  1. Slice the frankfurters into slices about 1cm wide. Slice the celery and dice the onion. Fry the onion and celery in a non-stick pan until soft.
  2. Add 1 tbsb flour, coat the vegetables in it and cook for about a minute.
  3. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly to mix in the flour. The mixture will thicken as it cooks.
  4. After a few minues you should have a smooth sauce. Add the sweetcorn (including the liquid from the tin), frankfurters and stock cube. Add more milk if it gets too thick.
  5. Once the frankfurters are heated through, add cayenne pepper to taste.
