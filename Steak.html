Bootcamp Cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye or sirloin steak (2–3 cm thick)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme

Instructions

  1. Remove the steak from the fridge 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Pat the steak dry and season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a pan over high heat and add olive oil.
  4. Place the steak in the pan and sear for 2–3 minutes per side.
  5. Add butter, garlic, and herbs to the pan.
  6. Baste the steak with the melted butter for another minute.
  7. Remove from heat and rest the steak for 5 minutes.
  8. Slice and serve.
