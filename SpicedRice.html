Simple spiced rice

Ingredients

  • 75g basmati rice
  • 1 tsp graram masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 130ml water

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and drain it.
  2. Place spices in the rice cooker and let it heat up.
  3. Once heated, add the rice and stir to coat the rice with spices.
  4. Add water, place the lid on and leave the rice cooker to complete its cooking.
  5. Once cooked, serve!
