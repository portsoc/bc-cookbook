Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix minced beef, onion, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  3. Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the burgers for 3â€“4 minutes per side.
  5. Add cheese on top during the last minute if using.
  6. Toast the buns lightly.
  7. Assemble the burger with your favourite toppings.
