Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 150g flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 200g butter

Method

  1. Preheat overn to 180 degrees Celsius
  2. Combine all ingredients
  3. Pour into 6 inch (15cm) cake tin
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes
  5. Cool on wire rack
  6. Decorate as preferred
