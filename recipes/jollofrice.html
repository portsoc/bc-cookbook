Jollof rice

Ingredients

  • Long grain parboiled rice
  • Tin chopped tomatoes
  • Seasoning Cube
  • 2 red bell pepper
  • 6 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 garlic
  • 2 tomato puree
  • Chicken stock
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend the tomatoes, red pepper, scotch bonnet, half the onion, seasoning cube and garlic until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and fry the remaining chopped onion until soft.
  3. Add the tomato puree and fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the blended mixture and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes until reduced and oil rises to the top.
  5. Add curry powder, thyme, bay leaves, and salt. Stir well.
  6. Add the stock and bring to a boil.
  7. Add the rice, stir once, then reduce heat to low.
  8. Cover tightly with foil and lid, cook for 30-35 minutes without stirring.
  9. Check if rice is cooked and water absorbed. If needed, add a little water and cook for 5 more minutes.
  10. Once cooked, stir and serve
